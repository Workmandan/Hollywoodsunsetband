Hollywood Sunset

Hollywood Sunset is a neighborhood band from northwest Fort Collins. Noah Cecil-Bruce and his father Skip and the Workman brothers, Peter and Daniel, make up the four-piece outfit. In keeping with their earlier folk-rock project, Winchester Holiday, the band takes inspiration from musicians such as Townes Van Zandt, Joni Mitchell, and Levon Helm, and paints American landscapes, sings of loves lost, and recounts childhood drama through guitar-infused harmony.
